overview

Feedeli is a food delivery app that integrates AI to understand users’ eating habits and preferences.

/ problem /

1.

Gen Y and Gen Z in big cities of Vietnam don't have enough time for cooking because they live a busy life with too much pressure.

2.

Experience with current food delivery apps in Vietnam makes it difficult for users to make decisions. Because the UI is carrying too many functions that make users distracted.

/ product idea /

A food delivery app designed as a “culinary assistant” which helps optimize every meal choice.

Designing an app with function of automatically remembering and learning user's preferences and habits to suggest restaurants close to their needs.
