overview

Wentilabs is a web app for creating virtual assistants and designing workflows. As a combination of generative AI technology and work management software, Wentilabs brings a new, convenient management solution for small and medium business teams.

/ problem /

1.

Chatbots allow businesses to automatically handle repetitive customer queries, but their effectiveness is often limited by pre-programmed responses.

2.

Small and medium-sized businesses often face challenges in organizing data and creating efficient workflows, which can lead to inconsistent data access and slower operations across departments.

/ product idea /

An application based on generative AI to create AI assistant and build workflow.
