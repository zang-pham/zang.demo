brand story

Hoi An Discovery Marathon, hosted by Race Jungle, offers an unique and scenic route through Hoi An’s highlights, including its old town, river, and peninsula. Participants can enjoy breathtaking views while aiming to complete the marathon.

Challenge

The visual identity for Hoi An Discovery Marathon aims to intimately and simply convey Hoi An's charm, differentiating from typical Vietnamese marathons. The proposed identity focuses on Hoi An's architectural beauty and iconic materials.

Logomark

The logo draws inspiration from yin-yang tiled roofs and diamond-shaped window frames typical in Hoi An architecture. These windows symbolize exploration and viewing, aligning with the race’s discovery theme.

Logotype

The logotype, customized from the bold, traditional Vietnamese font Hai Ba Trung, features high, narrow letters with elaborate tails and sharp curves. This font evokes Hoi An's ancient architecture, including pillars, roofs, and window frames.

The phrase "Discovery Marathon" is stylistically cut to create diamond-shaped spaces, evoking the image of diamond-shaped windows on walls.
