overview

Mindme is a website that sums up knowledge about depression and the experiences of people who have undergone this disorder.

Visit live site

The goal is to introduce viewers to the intricate inner world of individuals dealing with depression.

/ Design process /

/ visual system /

logo

The letter M-shaped logo is stylized from brain heatmaps.
One of the indicators for depression is the brain heatmap. Warm colors represent happy emotions, while blue colors represent sad emotions.

Typeface

Color

Blue is often associated with sadness and coldness. Black and White are combined to recreate the gloomy world that lacks of vivid colors of emotions in the inner world of depression patients.
